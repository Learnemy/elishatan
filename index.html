About

As the Developer Programs Manager for APAC at Facebook, I helped launch the Developer Circles program globally. Within a year, I've built communities across Asia to reach 40,000 (and counting!) developers, upskilling and helping them with employment and startup opportunities.

I've also founded TechLadies - a community-led initiative for women in Asia to connect, learn, and advance as programmers. TechLadies has over 2,400 members and taught 442 ladies programming skills. 9 ladies without any prior programming background received technical internships or hired as junior software engineers through our TechLadies Bootcamp.

Prior to Facebook & TechLadies, I picked up programming to start my startup called Learnemy, an online skills marketplace.

I've been invited to speak around Asia at conferences such as Slush Singapore, MaGIC Academy Symposium, RubyConf MY, SRECon Asia, and Techsauce Summit to name a few, and have mentored at Startup Weekends (San Francisco & Penang) and SPARK Global Acceleration Program. I've been featured in CNBC, The Straits Times, Her World, e27, High Net Worth, and more.

I'm based in the Sunny Singapore and aspire to also be a standup comedian.
